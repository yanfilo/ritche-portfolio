03

Dental Practice — Marketing Operations Execution Plan

Situation

A dental practice needed a clearer way to manage marketing execution across agency campaigns, GHL, funnel activity, front desk handoffs, and leadership reporting. The main challenge was not only generating leads, but understanding where qualified leads were getting delayed, dropped, or failing to convert into booked and shown patients.

What Was Built

Developed a 13-slide Marketing Operations Execution Plan that mapped the patient journey, diagnosed common conversion friction points, defined the coordination layer, and created practical tracker frameworks for agency deliverables, funnel QA, front desk experience, conversion issues, action items, and weekly leadership summaries.

Tools Used
PowerPoint Google Sheets Canva Notion
Capability Shown

The ability to translate an agency brief into a structured marketing operations system that connects strategy, CRM and funnel visibility, team accountability, and leadership decision-making. The deliverable shows how marketing activity can be organized into clear trackers, QA checks, action items, and reporting views that support better patient conversion.
